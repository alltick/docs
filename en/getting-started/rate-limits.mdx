---
title: 'Rate Limits'
description: 'API call frequency and quota limits by subscription plan'
---

AllTick enforces rate limits based on your subscription plan. Exceeding limits returns a 429 error code.

## REST API rate limits

| Plan | /kline | /batch-kline | /depth-tick | /trade-tick | Daily cap |
|------|--------|--------------|-------------|-------------|-----------|
| Free | 1 per 10s | 1 per 10s | 1 per 10s | 1 per 10s | 1,000 |
| Basic | 1 per 1s | 1 per 3s | 1 per 1s | 1 per 1s | 86,400 |
| Advanced | 10 per 1s | 1 per 2s | 10 per 1s | 10 per 1s | 864,000 |
| Professional | 20 per 1s | 1 per 1s | 20 per 1s | 20 per 1s | 1,728,000 |

## K-line query limits

| Endpoint | Max per request |
|----------|-----------------|
| `/kline` | 500 bars |
| `/batch-kline` | 2 bars × N products |

`/batch-kline` max product groups:

| Plan | Max groups |
|------|------------|
| Free | 5 |
| Basic | 100 |
| Advanced | 200 |
| Professional | 500 |

## WebSocket limits

| Rule | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Heartbeat frequency | Send every 10 seconds |
| Timeout disconnect | Connection closed after 30 seconds without heartbeat |
| Reconnection | Clients should implement automatic reconnection logic |

## IP restrictions

REST API rate limits are enforced per token only. There are no IP-based restrictions.
