## Product Categories

### Forex

Common codes: `EURUSD`, `USDJPY`, `GBPUSD`, `AUDUSD`, `USDCAD`, `USDCHF`

### Cryptocurrency

Common codes: `BTCUSDT`, `ETHUSDT`, `BNBUSDT`, `XRPUSDT`, `SOLUSDT`

### Precious Metals & Commodities

Common codes: `GOLD`, `SILVER`, `USOIL`, `UKOIL`

### Hong Kong Stocks

Format: `{code}.HK`

Examples: `700.HK` (Tencent), `9988.HK` (Alibaba), `1299.HK` (AIA)

### US Stocks

Format: `{code}.US`

Examples: `AAPL.US` (Apple), `GOOGL.US` (Google), `TSLA.US` (Tesla)

### A-Shares (China)

Format: `{code}.SH` or `{code}.SZ`

Examples: `600519.SH` (Kweichow Moutai), `000858.SZ` (Wuliangye)

<Note>
For the complete product code list, visit [AllTick website](https://alltick.co/). Code values are case-sensitive and must match the product list exactly.
</Note>
